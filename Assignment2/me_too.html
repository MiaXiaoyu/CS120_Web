My Favorite Restaurant

ALTIUS is my favorite restaurant. Rated Four Diamond by AAA, Altius is one of the top restaurants in Pittsburgh. Located on Mount Washington, it's hard to imagine how wonderful it is to enjoy a date while admiring the night view. I must say their filet mignon is absolutely delicious!

My Favorite Food

My favorite food is definitely hot pot. I can recommend a great hot pot restaurant in New York: Chongqing Laozao. It's a soup-based style where you can choose different flavored broths, then various ingredients including meats, seafood, and vegetables. You place them in the pot according to your preference, wait a bit for them to cook, and then enjoy. It's incredibly delicious. Hot pot is not only tasty but also a social experience -- sitting around the pot with friends and family, chatting while enjoying the food is my favorite way to dine.

My Favorite Books

Book Title Cover Author Summary
The Temple of Earth and Me Shi Tiesheng Shi Tiesheng's essays hit hard. He turns a quiet park into this profound space for wrestling with pain and meaning. Basically, disability writing before it was a genre.
A Room of One's Own Virginia Woolf Woolf's classic, but I finally got it this read. "500 pounds and a lock on the door" isn't just about writing, it's about women being allowed to think without interruption. Still relevant, sadly.
Oyster With Shell Is the Heart of the Adult Ni Ni Cute drawings, heavy feels. It's like the author peeled back adulthood to show all the soft, bruised parts we pretend aren't there. Made me want to call my mom.
Lychees of Chang'an Ma Boyong Loved how this book made a single lychee delivery feel like a thriller -- Tang Dynasty logistics were brutal. Low-key a critique of how systems grind people down, but with juicy historical details
