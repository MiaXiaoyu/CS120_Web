Meet Nemo's Echo

"Together is a beautiful place to be" - Creating magical moments through music, one wedding at a time

Our Musical Journey

"Our love story begins from here" - and so does yours

Nemo's Echo started three years ago as a student club from various Boston area schools. We were just a group of friends who loved music and wanted to perform together on weekends.

What began as small gigs at local coffee shops and student events has grown into something bigger. We've successfully performed at over 50 events, including many weddings where couples chose us because we could play their favorite songs.

We're still students, but we take our music seriously. We practice regularly at our studio and are always learning new songs that couples request. Our specialty is making popular songs sound great for weddings, whether it's acoustic for ceremonies or full band for dancing.

The name "Nemo's Echo" came from one of our late-night practice sessions. We liked how it sounded and how it represents music carrying across time, just like how wedding songs become part of people's memories.

Meet the Musicians

Xiaoyu Xia

Lead Vocalist & Guitar

Xiaoyu's voice has been compared to a mix of Taylor Swift and Norah Jones. She's our main vocalist and can make "Love Story" sound like it was written just for your wedding.

Mike Lee

Lead Guitar & Vocals

Mike brings the perfect blend of acoustic romance and electric energy to our performances. He's arranged many songs for wedding ceremonies and receptions.

Emma Wang

Piano & Keyboard

Emma is our secret weapon for emotional moments. Her piano arrangements of ballads have made people cry at weddings. She handles all our classical ceremony music with equal grace.

Alex Kim

Drums & Percussion

Alex keeps the energy alive and knows exactly when to bring the party. He reads the room perfectly and manages all our sound equipment to ensure clear audio.
